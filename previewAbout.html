Who we are

Our core development team consists of five kick-ass back-end developers and one front-end developer. Without those guys it wouldn't make much sense for our cool business-savvy product management and marketing guys to plot out new concepts and ideas. We always strive to integrate and utilize the newest technologies and best practices to deliver the best results to our customers. If you think you’re cool enough to join our group, we would be pleased to hear from you!

The Spirit

Mission Statement

Using web applications isn’t rocket science, and it shouldn’t feel like it; that’s why we do the math for users. We try hard to give users a simple, intuitive interface where they instantly feel comfortable and know what they can do with it. In user design, we focus on accessibility, scalability, and simplicity. To be prepared for the future we’re always anxious to refine and improve our framework. We face diverse challenges in areas like code quality or deployment with professional solutions and state-of-the-art web technologies, but are up to meet these challenges and constantly evolve our interfaces using recent best practices.
